What is China Monitor?

China Monitor is a comprehensive real-time dashboard that aggregates and displays critical information about China, including news, weather forecasts, and stock market data.

Features

  • Live News Aggregation - Curated news from Google News
  • Weather Forecasts - 3-day forecasts for 34 Chinese provinces
  • Real-time Stocks - A-Shares, H-Shares, and T-Shares
  • Beijing Time Display - Current time in China (UTC+8)
  • Dark/Light Theme - Switch between themes

Data Sources

  • News: Google News + SCMP
  • Weather: wttr.in + NMC
  • Stocks: TradingView Widget
